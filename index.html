How virtual try-on works

Our MediaPipe-powered face tracker maps 478 landmarks in real time and places each frame precisely on your face.

1

Pick a frame

Browse the catalog and choose any pair. Each model is rendered in 3D with physically-based materials.

2

Open your camera

Allow camera access (or upload a photo). Our tracker detects eyes, nose bridge and head orientation.

3

Try, adjust, capture

Fine-tune fit with zoom & rotation controls, then download a PNG of your favourite look.
